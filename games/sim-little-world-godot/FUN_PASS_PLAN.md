# Little World: Genesis — Fun Systems Pass

This second upgrade adds short-term decisions, discoveries, and replay rewards between the campaign milestones.

1. **Expedition Score** — reward healthy stewardship, completed research, discoveries, and mission progress.
2. **Diversity Combos** — using different tools in quick succession builds a multiplier and refunds Catalyst.
3. **Optional Field Studies** — rotating mini-objectives provide frequent, achievable goals outside the campaign.
4. **Research Hotspots** — glowing survey zones grant double Field Study progress when the correct tool is used there.
5. **Named Discoveries** — evolutionary and atmospheric milestones reveal procedurally named life discoveries.
6. **Creature Inspector** — right-click a nearby organism to pause and inspect its energy, generation, and evolved traits.
7. **Action Effects** — each intervention produces a restrained pulse and particle response at the target location.
8. **Crisis Coach** — the game detects starvation, overcrowding, overheating, and predator imbalance, then suggests a recovery action.
9. **Achievement Badges** — skillful play unlocks persistent run badges such as Chain Reaction and Steady Hands.
10. **World Grade + Endless Mode** — completing the campaign earns a C-to-S stewardship grade and allows continued sandbox play.

These systems are intentionally layered on top of the existing campaign rather than replacing it: campaign missions teach the game, Field Studies create moment-to-moment goals, and score/discoveries make each seed worth replaying.
